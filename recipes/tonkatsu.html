Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu is pork cutlet using a Japanese style breading.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Vegetable oil
  1. Beat the egg
  2. Dip the pork in all-purpose flour
  3. Dip the pork in the egg
  4. Dip the pork in the panko crumbs
  5. Fry the pork in vegetable oil and make whatever sides you want
