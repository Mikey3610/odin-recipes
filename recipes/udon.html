Udon

Udon is a thick noodle made from wheat flour, used in Japanese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Negi (Japanese long onion)
  • Dashi
  • Condiments
  1. Prepare the dashi broth
  2. Stir fry the beef and negi
  3. Cook the udon noodles
  4. Assemble the noodles and broth together into a soup
